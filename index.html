About Us

RiverSight Systems is dedicated to identifying pollution in any body of water through the use of AI-driven computer vision technology. Our mission is to support those committed to cleaning polluted waters by providing data-driven insights that help identify the most productive methods for cleaning. Additionally, we aim to raise public awareness of water pollution issues, empowering communities to take meaningful action towards a cleaner, healthier environment.
